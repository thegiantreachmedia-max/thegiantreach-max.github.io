Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026 · The Giant Reach · Opeyemi Akinremi

The Giant Reach ("we", "us", "our") is operated by Opeyemi Akinremi, based in Lagos, Nigeria. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use thegiantreach.com or interact with our services.

1. Who We Are

The Giant Reach is a client acquisition systems agency founded by Opeyemi Akinremi, Lagos, Nigeria. Contact: opeyemi@thegiantreach.com · Website: thegiantreach.com

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following when you interact with our website or services:

  • Name, phone number, WhatsApp number, and email address — submitted via contact forms, WhatsApp, or email
  • Business type, country, and specific acquisition challenges — submitted via qualification forms
  • Anonymised website usage data via Google Analytics 4 (GA4) and Google Tag Manager (GTM)
  • Cookies for analytics and ad performance attribution (Google Ads, Meta Ads)

We do not collect payment card details directly through this website.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to enquiries and provide the services you've requested
  • To deliver and manage client acquisition system retainers
  • To improve our website and services based on usage analytics
  • To comply with applicable legal obligations

We never sell your personal data to third parties.

4. WhatsApp Communications

By messaging us on WhatsApp, you consent to receiving responses from The Giant Reach and Opeyemi Akinremi via the WhatsApp platform. WhatsApp is operated by Meta Platforms, Inc., and is governed by their separate privacy policy.

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services which have their own privacy policies:

  • Google Analytics 4 & Tag Manager — website analytics and event tracking
  • Google Ads — paid search advertising and conversion tracking
  • Meta (Facebook/Instagram Ads) — paid social advertising
  • Calendly — meeting scheduling
  • WhatsApp (Meta) — messaging communications
  • Cloudinary — image hosting

6. Cookies

We use cookies for Google Analytics and advertising attribution. You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect your browsing experience on this site.

7. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely and used only for business communication and service delivery purposes. We use industry-standard security practices to protect your information from unauthorised access. Blog posts published through the admin panel are stored in your browser's localStorage and are not transmitted to our servers.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to processing of your data
  • Request data portability

To exercise any of these rights, email opeyemi@thegiantreach.com. We respond within 30 days.

9. Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such data, please contact us immediately.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of our website or services after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy. The "last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect the most recent revision.

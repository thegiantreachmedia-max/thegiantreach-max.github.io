The Problem Without a CRM

Immigration leads are falling through cracks
your team can't even see.

Without a CRM, applicant enquiries exist across WhatsApp threads, email inboxes, sticky notes, and someone's memory. Serious applicants fall through the cracks while your team is focused on active cases. There's no visibility on which stage each lead is at, who is responsible for following up, or which leads came from which channel.

  • Leads forgotten between first contact and consultation
  • No visibility on pipeline value or conversion rate
  • Team members duplicating or missing follow-ups
  • No data on which marketing channels produce paying clients
  • Revenue unpredictable because pipeline is invisible

The Pipeline Structure

Every applicant tracked through
every stage of your process.

Stage 1
New Enquiry
Lead enters the pipeline. Whether from a form, WhatsApp message, or phone call, every new enquiry is captured immediately in the CRM with source, date, and initial contact details. Automated entry where possible.
Stage 2
Qualification
Applicant screened. Qualification form completed or manual review done. Lead scored and categorised. Unqualified leads handled automatically. Qualified leads moved forward with notes.
Stage 3
Contacted
First touchpoint made. Your team has reached out. Follow-up schedule set. Next action logged. Responsibility assigned to a specific consultant. Timeline tracked.
Stage 4
Consultation Booked
Consultation scheduled. Meeting booked via Calendly or manual arrangement. Date and time recorded. Pre-consultation checklist sent to applicant. Confirmation logged.
Stage 5
Proposal Sent
Engagement proposal or service quote sent. Value tracked. Follow-up scheduled. Decision timeline noted. Probability of conversion recorded.
Stage 6
Client Signed
Retainer signed. Client onboarded. Lead moves to active client status. Revenue recorded. Source attribution confirmed. Pipeline metrics updated for reporting.
Stage 7
Lost / Archived
Applicant not converting — reason recorded. Lost reason tracked (price, eligibility, went elsewhere, no response). Data feeds into monthly optimisation. Nothing disappears without analysis.

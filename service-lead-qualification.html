The Problem

Immigration consultants are burning time
on leads that were never going to convert.

Without a qualification system, every enquiry that comes in gets treated as a potential client — regardless of eligibility, budget, or seriousness. Your consultants spend hours in consultations with applicants who are ineligible, can't afford your services, or are just shopping around.

That consultant time has a real cost. And every hour spent with an unqualified applicant is an hour not spent converting a serious one.

  • Consultants booked with ineligible applicants who can't proceed
  • Time wasted on budget mismatches discovered only in consultation
  • No way to prioritise high-probability leads over low-probability ones
  • Every lead treated equally regardless of conversion potential
  • Marketing metrics show many leads but conversion rate stays low
The Solution

Automated scoring that sorts leads
before they reach your team.

The Giant Reach builds custom qualification systems for immigration agencies — forms, logic, and scoring that assess every enquiry automatically based on the criteria that matter for your specific visa categories and services.

  • Custom form built around your specific visa categories
  • Automatic scoring based on eligibility signals
  • Three-tier routing: Hot (book consultation), Warm (nurture), Cold (decline politely)
  • Automated responses to each tier — no manual sorting needed
  • Integration with your CRM and lead capture flow
  • Reduces unqualified consultations by 60–80%
How It Works

The Qualification System Process

📋
Step 1 — Applicant Submits Qualification Form
After clicking an ad or visiting your website, the applicant completes a short assessment form covering: visa category interest, current location and passport, employment/financial status, timeline, and previous visa history. Designed to feel helpful, not interrogative.
⚙️
Step 2 — Automatic Scoring Runs
The system scores each response against your eligibility criteria. High-scoring answers (right visa category, eligible country, right timeline, right budget range) push the score up. Disqualifying factors (wrong passport for that visa route, clear ineligibility) push it down or trigger an automatic decline.
🔀
Step 3 — Lead Routed to Correct Tier
Based on score: Hot leads (high eligibility, serious intent) are routed immediately to book a consultation. Warm leads (potential but needs nurturing) are sent to a follow-up sequence. Cold leads (ineligible or mismatched) receive a polite, pre-written response directing them appropriately.
🗂️
Step 4 — CRM Updated Automatically
Every lead is logged in your CRM with their score, tier, and form responses. Your team opens the CRM and sees only the leads that have passed qualification — with the context they need to have a productive first conversation. No manual sorting required.
🔥 Hot Lead
High Score — Book Immediately
Eligible applicant, clear intent, right budget, strong timeline. Routed directly to consultation booking. Automated WhatsApp or email confirmation sent. Consultant notified.
⚡ Warm Lead
Moderate Score — Nurture Sequence
Potential applicant, some eligibility signals, unclear timeline or budget. Enters a follow-up email/WhatsApp sequence. Invited to re-qualify in 30–60 days when ready to proceed.
❄️ Cold Lead
Low Score — Politely Declined
Ineligible for current service, significant barriers to proceeding. Receives a respectful auto-response explaining the mismatch. No consultant time consumed. Lead archived in CRM with reason.

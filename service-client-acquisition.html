Why Most Immigration Agencies Stay Stuck

The problem isn't that you're not spending on marketing.
It's that there's no system around the spending.

Here is what Opeyemi Akinremi at The Giant Reach finds in almost every immigration agency audit: there is some spending on ads, some traffic coming in, some leads arriving via WhatsApp or email — but nothing connecting them. No landing page matched to the ad. No CRM tracking where the lead came from. No follow-up system. No data showing which campaigns actually produce paying clients.

The Six-Component System

How the Client Acquisition System
works for immigration agencies

Built and managed by Opeyemi Akinremi at The Giant Reach. Every component serves the next. No gaps in the pipeline.

01
Traffic
Google Ads & Meta Ads for Immigration Intent
Targeting people actively searching for visa and immigration services — not people who might be interested, but people who are searching right now. For a UK visa consultancy that means keywords like "skilled worker visa UK" and "UK immigration consultant." For a Nigerian agency helping clients reach Canada, it means "Canada PR application Nigeria." Every campaign is built around buyer intent, not impressions or brand awareness. The goal is enquiries from serious applicants.
02
Qualification
Lead Qualification Before They Reach Your Team
Every campaign is paired with a qualification form that screens applicants by eligibility, seriousness, and visa category before they reach your consultants. This eliminates time-wasters from the pipeline early, so your team only speaks to applicants who are genuinely eligible and ready to move forward. The qualification system scores each applicant automatically and routes them accordingly.
03
Conversion
Dedicated Immigration Landing Pages
Traffic without conversion is wasted spend. Every campaign I build is matched to a dedicated landing page — not a homepage, not a services page. A single page built specifically to convert an immigration enquiry. One headline that mirrors the ad, one clear value proposition, one CTA — whether a WhatsApp link, Calendly booking form, or qualification form. No navigation bar. No links to other pages. The visitor arrived with intent. The page has one job.
04
Tracking
Full GA4 + Conversion Tracking
GA4 with conversion events tracking every enquiry channel — WhatsApp link clicks, form submissions, Calendly bookings, phone call clicks. Every lead is traceable back to the specific ad, keyword, and campaign that generated it. You always know your cost per qualified enquiry, your best-performing campaigns, and where to put more budget. No more guessing. No more spending and hoping.
05
Pipeline
Notion CRM Pipeline for Applicant Management
Every enquiry enters a structured Notion CRM pipeline with defined stages — from first contact through consultation booked, documentation submitted, and retainer signed. Every applicant is visible. Every team member knows where each prospect stands. No lead falls through the cracks because someone forgot to follow up. The pipeline is your immigration agency's single source of truth for active acquisition.
06
Automation + Optimisation
24/7 Follow-Up & Monthly Improvements
Automated follow-up workflows built with Make and Pipedream respond to every new enquiry within minutes — day or night, weekend or holiday. An applicant who fills in a form at 10pm on a Sunday hears from your agency before they contact the next consultant on their list. Every month, Opeyemi Akinremi reviews campaign data, optimises landing pages, refines follow-up sequences, and delivers a plain-English performance report. The system improves every cycle.

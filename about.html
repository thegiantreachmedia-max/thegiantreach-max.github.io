Who I Am

Building systems.
Not just running ads.

I'm Opeyemi Akinremi — founder of The Giant Reach, a client acquisition systems agency specialising in immigration agencies, visa consultants, and study abroad businesses.

I understand immigration businesses personally. I know what it means to want to build a life somewhere better. I know the weight of trust a family places in the consultancy guiding them through that journey — and I know how damaging it is when that consultancy loses a serious applicant simply because their acquisition system was broken.

That's what I fix.

My work centres on one thing: building complete acquisition systems that connect traffic, lead qualification, CRM, tracking, and automation into one unified pipeline — so immigration businesses generate consistent, measurable enquiries every month.

Most marketers manage one part of the process. I build the entire system. From the first Google search to the signed client agreement — every stage is designed, connected, and measured.

If your acquisition is not structured — it cannot scale.
If it is not tracked — it cannot improve.

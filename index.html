The System

How The Client Acquisition System Works

Six steps. One pipeline. Built to run every month so your business generates clients consistently — not randomly.

01
Traffic
Google Ads & Meta Ads
I bring in high-intent leads using Google Ads and Meta Ads — structured campaigns built around buyer intent, not vanity metrics. The right keywords, the right audience, the right bid strategy.
02
Conversion
Landing Pages That Convert
I build dedicated landing pages that turn clicks into enquiries. One message, one CTA, no distractions. No more sending paid traffic to a homepage and hoping for the best.
03
Tracking
Full Tracking via GA4
Every lead is tracked using GA4 and conversion systems. You always know where your clients came from, what it cost to acquire them, and what's working.
04
Pipeline
CRM Setup (Notion)
Leads are organised into a structured CRM pipeline so your team always knows where each prospect stands. No leads slip through the cracks.
05
Follow-up
Automation (Make / Pipedream)
Automated follow-up workflows ensure no lead goes cold. The system works for your business 24/7 — even when you're not at your desk.
06
Optimization
Monthly Reporting & Improvement
Every month you get a clean report — leads generated, cost per lead, pipeline value. The system is then optimized based on real data, not guesswork.

This is not a service. It is a system that runs every month to produce predictable client flow.

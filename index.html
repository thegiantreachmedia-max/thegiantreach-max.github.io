🧊 Simulation · Google Ads · UK Market
ClearFlow Plumbing Solutions
London, UK  ·  General Plumbing · Drain Clearance · Water Heater Installation
Problems Identified
Low-Quality Leads & Irrelevant Clicks
Ads attracting DIY searches, job seekers, and free-advice queries. No negative keywords (-DIY, -how-to, -free, -jobs) meant spend was going to clicks that would never convert.
Poor Conversion Tracking
No system to capture enquiries. Couldn't measure which clicks led to actual paying jobs — zero visibility on ROI.
Weak Ad Copy & Low-Intent CTAs
Generic ads with no clear calls-to-action. People clicked but didn't submit forms — wasted spend and low lead conversion rate.
How It Was Fixed
1
High-Intent Keyword Targeting
Researched UK-specific keywords: "water heater installation London," "drain clearance London," "general plumbing services London." CPC ranges used to manage budget upfront.
Knowing CPC ranges means budget is allocated where it produces real enquiries, not vanity clicks.
2
Benefit-Driven Ad Copy & CTAs
Headlines: "Book Your Plumbing Service Today!" / "Water Heater Install in 24hrs — Call Now." Descriptions drove form submissions and calls.
Clicks now convert into submissions or calls — not dead bounces.
3
Conversion Tracking via Booking Page
Clicks directed to landing page with booking form and call button. Tracking snippet added to the confirmation page for accurate data.
Only real, actionable leads are counted → ROI measurement is accurate.
4
Campaign Structure & Local Targeting
Campaign 1 – Plumbing Services (Water Heater · Drain Clearance). Campaign 2 – General Enquiries. 60 km radius around London.
Each service gets its own budget and targeting → max ROI per intent.

🧊 Simulation · Google Ads · Canada Market
NorthPeak Heating & Cooling
North York, Toronto, Canada  ·  Emergency Furnace Repair · AC Installation · Maintenance
Problems Identified
Overspending on Low-Intent Traffic
Competing on broad keywords against top Toronto HVAC brands meant budget was burning on searchers who weren't ready to book — driving up CPC without improving lead quality.
No Call Tracking for Qualified Leads
Zero call tracking in existing campaigns. No way to know which ads drove real service calls vs. price shoppers or wrong numbers.
How It Was Fixed
1
Competitor Research
Captured top 4 Google Ads positions for target keywords to analyse messaging, positioning, and competitive gaps.
Proves broad keywords were costing money without quality leads — and reveals angles competitors aren't using.
2
High-Intent Keyword Research
Targeted: "emergency furnace repair toronto," "furnace repair near me," "24 hour heating repair toronto." Monthly searches and CPC ranges collected for all targets.
Realistic CPC benchmarks justify the budget and show exactly where ROI will come from.
3
Intent-Based Campaign Structure
Campaign 1 – Emergency Repair (Furnace Emergency · 24hr Heating). Campaign 2 – Installation (Furnace Install). Each with its own budget.
Structure by intent → better budget allocation and higher ROAS.
4
Hyper-Local Targeting + Negative Keywords
20 km radius around Toronto. Negatives: free, DIY, jobs, cheap, training — eliminating irrelevant traffic before it spends a dollar.
No wasted impressions. Every click is a potential booking.
5
Qualified Call Tracking Setup
Conversions set for calls longer than 60 seconds — filtering out wrong numbers so only real, qualified leads are measured.
ROI is measured on actual bookings, not noise.
